CRAP Website Evaluation

The Big Ugly Website

The Big Ugly Website is a satirical collection showcasing some of the most poorly designed and aesthetically unpleasing websites on the internet. It serves as a humorous archive, highlighting examples of bad web design for educational and entertainment purposes.

  • Contrast: The site intentionally lacks visual contrast, with clashing colors and unreadable text, exemplifying poor design choices.
  • Repetition: There is a deliberate absence of cohesive repetition; inconsistent fonts, colors, and layouts are used to mock bad design practices.
  • Alignment: The website features misaligned elements, with text and images placed haphazardly, demonstrating the chaos of poor alignment.
  • Proximity: Related items are scattered randomly, ignoring the principle of grouping similar elements together, which adds to the site's intentionally confusing layout.

Clarity in Crazy

ClarityInCrazy.com is the official website for Ok Drugs, a company specializing in vegan, THC-free CBD gummies known as "Peach Eatables." The site offers information about their products, company background, and related merchandise.

  • Contrast: The website employs a cohesive color scheme with sufficient contrast between text and background, ensuring readability and a pleasant visual experience.
  • Repetition: Consistent use of fonts, colors, and design elements across all pages reinforces the brand identity and provides a unified user experience.
  • Alignment: Elements on the site are well-aligned, with text, images, and navigation menus properly positioned to create a structured and organized layout.
  • Proximity: Related items and information are grouped together logically, aiding in intuitive navigation and helping users find relevant content with ease.
