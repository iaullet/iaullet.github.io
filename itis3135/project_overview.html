Project Overview

Project Name

Risk IQ Consulting

Application Purpose

The application will provide a platform for Risk IQ to promote its risk management consulting services and provide a means of contact for potential clients. Users will be able to learn more about the company, its founder and the services offered. Each service will be broken down into brief descriptions to help users determine their needs. Finally, they will be able to contact the company with any questions, concerns or insights. This application will help grow clientele for the company, increase opportunities for partnerships and track statistics(ex. conversion rate).

Intended Users

Prospective Clients (B2B)

Businesses, financial institutions, and corporate leaders seeking risk management solutions for their operations.

  • CEOs, CFOs, and decision-makers at financial firms.
  • Managers of compliance and risk departments in banks, insurance companies, or investment firms.
  • Executives of small to mid-size companies in various industries who need financial risk strategies.

Partners

Other firms or consultants that may collaborate with the LLC in providing services or referrals.

  • Financial audit firms.
  • Legal or regulatory consulting firms.
  • Technology companies providing tools for risk assessment.

Existing Clients

Current or past clients that want to stay updated on services, outcomes, or ongoing projects.

  • Clients receiving risk management services, consulting, or customized strategies.
  • Businesses looking for reports, feedback, or project follow-ups.
