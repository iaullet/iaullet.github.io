About the Founder

Bringing Expertise, Precision, and Vision to Risk Management

Mario Aullet

Mario Aullet is a seasoned risk management professional with over 20 years of experience in the financial services industry. With expertise spanning operational, technology, cybersecurity, compliance, third party, AI, and strategic risks, he specializes in building strong control environments and driving smarter decision-making.

Throughout Mario's career, he's led major initiatives to strengthen control environments, guide risk and control self-assessments, and mitigate both operational and cybersecurity threats. His work includes:

  • Conducting Risk & Control Self-Assessments (RCSAs), audits, and targeted risk assessments
  • Building Process, Risk, and Control Inventories (PRC)
  • Designing, implementing, and testing effective control environments
  • Managing cybersecurity and operational risk
  • Leading issues management efforts and remediating control weaknesses

Mario has supported diverse business areas such as consumer deposits, home lending, commercial real estate, treasury services, and the payments space—spanning credit and debit cards, merchant services, mobile, ACH, RTP, and remittances.

Known for blending subject-matter expertise with data-driven insights, he regularly applies tools like Tableau, Power BI, Python, R, and Gen AI to enhance analysis and decision-making. Mario's approach reflects a commitment to speed, scalability, and risk intelligence tailored to each client's unique environment.

Certifications Include:

  • Certified in Risk and Information Systems Controls (CRISC)
  • Project Management Professional (PMP)
  • Agile Certified Practitioner (PMI-ACP)
  • Six Sigma Master Black Belt (CSSMBB)
  • Microsoft Azure Cloud Fundamentals (AZ-900)
