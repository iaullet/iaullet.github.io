Risk IQ Advisors LLC

Risk IQ Advisors LLC is a specialized risk management consulting firm dedicated to helping financial institutions, tech innovators, and small to medium-sized businesses make smarter risk decisions. Founded and led by a seasoned risk professional, our firm offers a unique, one-on-one advisory experience grounded in real-world expertise and a deep understanding of today's complex risk landscape. At Risk IQ, we believe that risk is not just something to manage—it's something to understand, anticipate, and turn into opportunity. That's why we work closely with every client to design and implement tailored strategies that go beyond compliance, creating real value through operational resilience and informed decision-making. We bring clarity to complex challenges by offering services that span:

  • Risk mitigation across operational, technological, cyber, compliance, and reputational domains
  • Control framework design and implementation
  • Risk and control self-assessments (RCSAs)
  • Issues and remediation project management
  • Process risk and control inventories for enterprise-wide visibility

Whether you're a financial institution facing regulatory demands, a startup building a risk function from the ground up, or a business navigating growth, Risk IQ Advisors is here to help you grow with confidence.
